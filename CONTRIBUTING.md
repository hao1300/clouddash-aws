# Contributing

Thanks for considering a contribution to CloudDash.

By submitting a contribution, you agree that your contribution is licensed under the same license terms as this repository, currently `FSL-1.1-ALv2`, including its future Apache-2.0 license grant for each released version.

Contributions are voluntary unless there is a separate written agreement. Sponsorships and commercial build revenue do not automatically create payment obligations for contributors.

Please keep contributions focused, avoid committing credentials or customer data, and do not include code or assets unless you have the right to contribute them under these terms.
